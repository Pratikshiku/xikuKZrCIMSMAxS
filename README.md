## University Employment Recruitment System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/022492874f6b4ac1b69e4c2335710b29/ghb20250916) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User, Enterprise, Counselor, Administrator.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Job Announcements
5. Employment Knowledge
6. Enterprise
7. Job Recruitment
8. Personal Information
9. Graduation Destination
10. Resume
11. Employment Knowledge Favorites
12. Job Favorites
13. Resume Submission
14. Employment Consultation


**Counselor Functions:**
1. Login
2. Change Password
3. Personal Information
4. Employment Consultation Management
5. Graduation Destination Management
6. Job Announcement Management
7. Resume Management
8. Job Recruitment Management
9. Job Message Management
10. Resume Submission Management
11. Employment Knowledge Management
12. Employment Knowledge Message Management
13. Enterprise Information

**Enterprise Functions:**
1. Login
2. Change Password
3. Personal Information
4. Job Announcement Information
5. Job Recruitment Management
6. Job Message Management
7. Resume Submission Management
8. Employment Knowledge Information




**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Employment Information Management
5. Basic Data Management (Graduation Destination Type Management, Job Announcement Type Management, Job Intention Management, Employment Knowledge Type Management, Enterprise Type Management, Recruitment Type Management, Recruitment Position Management)
6. Graduation Destination Management
7. Job Announcement Management
8. Resume Management
9. Job Recruitment Management
10. Resume Submission Management
11. Employment Knowledge Management
12. User Management (User Management, Counselor Management, Enterprise Management)
13. Carousel Management
